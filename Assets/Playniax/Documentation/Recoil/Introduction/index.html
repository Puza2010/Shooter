Recoil / Introduction

What is this?

Recoil is a game template for Unity.

You can change or add levels, change or add game mechanics, rename or reskin the game and publish it anywere you like.

Prologue

You are a sentient gun drifting through the vastness of space.

Across the universe, an alien race is spreading peace and love, illuminating the cosmos with their benevolence.

You crave this love and seek to capture it, holding onto its warmth amidst the cold void.

But beware, for other guns and mysterious objects also yearn for this love, and they will stop at nothing to claim it for themselves.

Navigate this perilous journey and embrace the love that shines in the darkness.



What do you get?

This package contains:

• A Game manager
• Demo level
• Demo assets

• The Ignition framework from Playniax

How to play?

Start the game by tapping the screen or clicking the mouse button.

Use your timing skills and gun rotation to move in the desired direction.

Collect hearts and bullets.

Complete each level by collecting all the hearts, but be careful not to run out of bullets.
